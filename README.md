# Full Stack Developer - SaaS Platform

**Additional Requirements:**  
- U.S. citizen  
- Must pass a background check  
- Remote role preferred, ideally EST or CST time zone  
- No sponsorship required  

**Role Overview:**  
Our client is seeking a Full Stack Developer to help design, build, and scale a cloud-based SaaS platform. Work across the stack on front-end UI, back-end services, cloud infrastructure, and more.  

**Top Skills Required:**  
1. Python  
2. Django  
3. PostgreSQL  
4. AWS  
5. Cursor AI exposure  

**Responsibilities:**  
- Design, build, and maintain end-to-end web applications  
- Develop responsive front-end interfaces (React, Vue, Angular)  
- Integrate third-party systems and APIs  
- Write clean, testable, well-documented code  
- Collaborate with product managers and designers  
- Maintain and monitor the cloud environment  
- Design and manage databases for performance and reliability  
- Contribute to CI/CD pipelines and DevOps processes  
- Monitor, troubleshoot, and resolve production issues  
- Stay current with emerging technologies and best practices  

**Desired Experience & Education:**  
- 6–8 years in Full Stack Development or similar role  
- Bachelor's in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience)  
- Experience in regulated industries preferred  
- Strong backend skills in Python/Django; Go or similar is a plus  
- Front-end proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript & frameworks (React, Vue, Angular)  
- Experience with RESTful APIs, cloud platforms (AWS preferred), relational & NoSQL databases  
- Familiarity with Git, CI/CD, Docker  
- Knowledge of security best practices  
- Agile experience and ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams  
- Excellent problem-solving, communication, and organizational skills  

**How to Apply:**  
Connect via LinkedIn or open an issue in this repo.
